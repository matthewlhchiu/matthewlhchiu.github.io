# Research projects
This space is a collection of the research and academic projects that I've worked on.

## Current research projects
- **Education and meritocracy in South Korea** (2024): I am currently researching the effect of South Korea's [high school equalization policy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High_school_standardization_policy_in_South_Korea) on beliefs in meritocracy. (*ongoing*)

## Past research projects
- **Production functions for the generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) industry** (2024): I analyzed the state of the generative AI industry and postulated economic assumptions and functional forms that can help model production of frontier generative AI systems.
- **State of national and international AI governance** (2023): As part of the Berkeley AI Safety Student Initiative, I contributed to a research report comparing and analysing national AI regulations to identify commonalities and disparities in AI governance, focusing on the UK and Latin America. I was supervised by Anka Reuel, a PhD student in Computer Science at Stanford University.
- **Climate change awareness and attitudes in Sub-Saharan Africa** (2023): As part of my BSc degree, my team and I conducted a research project to understand and identify the correlates of climate change awareness and attitudes in Sub-Saharan Africa. We presented our results and findings to the World Bank.
- **The causes of revolutions** (2023): I led an LSE team of undergraduates and postgraduates in determining the predictors of social unrest using machine learning and regression techniques in Python; project was recognised as the joint best project by academics from LSE's Data Science Institute. ([Presentation]({{ site.baseurl }}/files/Data Science Revolutions presentation.pdf))
- **Impact estimates of community building activities** (2022): I estimated the impact of community building work in effective altruism (EA) using [Squiggle](https://www.squiggle-language.com/), winning $1,200 as part of a challenge to quantify the impact of cause areas in EA. ([Report](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/gKywAZWEWe4WWfoEx/estimating-the-impact-of-community-building-work))
- **Risk factors of child abuse for ethnic minority communities** (2020–21): As part of a Polygeia research team, I conducted an extensive literature review of the prevalence and risk factors child abuse and maltreatment in ethnic minority communities.

## Essays
- **The sleeping beauty problem: defending the thirder** (2022): I researched the Sleeping Beauty problem in formal epistemology, vehemently defending the thirder perspective.
- **Women's political participation: what should the UN do?** (2022): I wrote about policies and interventions that UN Women should consider implementing to improve women's political participation in developing countries.
