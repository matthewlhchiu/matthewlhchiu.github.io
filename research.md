# Research
I use this space to collect my research projects.

## Current projects
- **Correlates of climate change awareness and attitudes in sub-Saharan Africa** (2023): For project to understand and identify the correlates of climate change awareness and attitudes in sub-Saharan Africa. As part of my BSc degree, my team and I presented our findings to the World Bank. (*ongoing.*)
- **State of national and international AI governance** (2023): I contributed to a research report comparing and analysing national AI regulations to identify commonalities and disparities in AI governance, focusing on the UK and Latin America. (*ongoing.*)

## Past projects
- **The causes of revolutions** (2023): I led an LSE team of undergraduates and postgraduates, we attempted to determine the predictors of social unrest using machine learning and regression techniques in Python. Our project was recognised as the joint best project by academics from LSE's Data Science Institute. ([Presentation]({{ site.baseurl }}/files/Data Science Revolutions presentation.pdf))
- **Impact estimates of community building activities** (2022): I researched and estimated the impact of community building work in effective altruism (EA) using [Squiggle](https://www.squiggle-language.com/), winning $1,200 as part of a challenge to quantify the impact of cause areas in EA. ([Report](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/gKywAZWEWe4WWfoEx/estimating-the-impact-of-community-building-work))
- **Risk factors of child abuse for ethnic minority communities** (2020–21): As part of a Polygeia research team, I conducted an extensive literature review of the prevalence and risk factors child abuse and maltreatment in ethnic minority communities. (*Report TBD.*)
