# Research
I use this space to collect my research projects.

2023: I led an LSE team of undergraduates and postgraduates, we attempted to determine the predictors of social unrest using machine learning and regression techniques in Python. Our project was recognised as the joint best project by academics from LSE's Data Science Institute. ([Presentation]({{ site.baseurl }}/files/Data Science Revolutions presentation.pdf))
2022: I researched and estimated the impact of community-building work in effective altruism (EA) using [Squiggle](https://www.squiggle-language.com/), winning $1,200 as part of a challenge to quantify the impact of cause areas in EA. ([Report](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/posts/gKywAZWEWe4WWfoEx/estimating-the-impact-of-community-building-work))
2021: As part of a Polygeia research team, I conducted an extensive literature review of the prevalence and risk factors child abuse and maltreatment in ethnic minority communities. (*Report TBD.*)
