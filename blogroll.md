# Blogroll

A favourite pastime of mine is to read widely. Reading is a wonderful activity serving the dual purpose of stimulating the mind and nourishing the soul.

I am lucky that most pieces I come across are often original, illuminating, and clarify one’s model of the world. But I have found time and time again that the very best pieces of writing either introduces new thoughts that alter one’s fundamental understanding of reality, leaving one with a refreshed sense of meaning, or reframes old ideas, leaving one with an unusual desire to interrogate and understand what one really thinks about these ideas. The intellectual joy from reading these very best pieces adds a third purpose to reading: there is a irreplaceable and special pleasure that comes with understanding, conversing, and interrogating the meaning behind the words.

I share some of my favourite writers, bloggers, commentators below. Most are original; some are exceptional and have changed me as a person, or some aspect of my worldview.
